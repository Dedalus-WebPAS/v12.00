Monthly Statistical Update

This program recreates Medical Practice/Doctor statistics from a given date range from items in the transaction file, i.e. all invoices raised, cash receipts and journals that have been put into the system. The financial figures for a month are replaced and subsequent opening balances are recalculated.

Please ensure that no private practice transactions are put through the system until the program has completed its run.
