This process will trim the data in a casemix extract. Trimming will remove extreme episodes from the casemix extract. Both the arithmetic mean and the standard deviation are affected considerably by the presence of even a relatively small number of very extreme cases. The quartile trimming method is based on the method used in the DRGs 1991-92 Report of the Victorian Health Department. Simple percentage trimming will identify the number of cases to exclude eg If 10 % of cases are to be trimmed from a group that contains 60 epiosdes then 6 episodes will be excluded, 3 from the top and 3 from the bottom of the list, sorted by the trim basis.
